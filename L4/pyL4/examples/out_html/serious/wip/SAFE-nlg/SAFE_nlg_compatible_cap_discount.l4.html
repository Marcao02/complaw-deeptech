Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE)

The parameters to the contract are:
  • PURCHASE_AMOUNT, which is a positive USD amount.
  • DISCOUNT_RATE, which is a fraction in the range (0,1].
  • VALUATION_CAP, which is a positive USD amount.

  • Scenario: Investment Agreement Signed

    • Investor may Transfer Investment to Company

  • Scenario: Investment Waiting to Convert

    • Company may Initiate an Equity Financing Event
    • Company may Initiate an IPO Liquidity Event
    • if ?total_investments_of_cashout_investors is at least PURCHASE_AMOUNT
      • Company may Initiate a Change-of-Control Liquidity Event
    • Company may Initiate a Dissolution Event

  • Investor action Transfer Investment to Company

      Define:
        investor_cash is:
        • ( investor_cash - PURCHASE_AMOUNT )
        company_cash is:
        • ( company_cash + PURCHASE_AMOUNT )

      Go to Investment Waiting to Convert

  • Equity Events

    • Scenario: After PRA Signed

      • Within 1 day of the last event:
        • Company may Issue SAFE Preferred Stock
      • After 1 day since the last event:
        • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Initiate an Equity Financing Event

      • Scenario:

        • Company may Deliver Transaction Docs With Pro Rata Rights Agreement (PRA)
        • Company may Deliver Transaction Docs (PRA to follow)
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Deliver Transaction Docs With Pro Rata Rights Agreement (PRA)

        Go to After PRA Signed

    • Company action Deliver Transaction Docs (PRA to follow)

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Send PRA for Signing
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Send PRA for Signing

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Investor may Sign PRA
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Investor breaches the contract.

    • Investor action Sign PRA

        Go to After PRA Signed

    • Company action Issue SAFE Preferred Stock

        Company must provide:
        • company_capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • premoney_valuation, which is a positive USD amount

        Define:
          conversion_price is:
          • the minimum of VALUATION_CAP / company_capitalization and premoney_valuation / company_capitalization * DISCOUNT_RATE
          investor_SAFE_Preferred_Stock is:
          • ( investor_SAFE_Preferred_Stock + ⎡ PURCHASE_AMOUNT / conversion_price'⎤ )

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Execute Equity Financing
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Execute Equity Financing

        Contract fulfilled.

  • Liquidity Events

    • Scenario: Investor to Choose Method of Payment

      • Before 1 week since the last event:
        • Investor may Choose Payment in Cash
      • Before 1 week since the last event:
        • Investor may Choose Payment in Common Stock
      • At 1 week since the last event:
        • Go to Stock Payment Option Chosen by Default

    • Company action Initiate an IPO Liquidity Event

        Company must provide:
        • company_cash_at_liquidity_event, which is a USD amount
        • liquidity_capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • company_valuation, which is a positive USD amount

        Define:
          conversion_price is:
          • the minimum of VALUATION_CAP / liquidity_capitalization and company_valuation / liquidity_capitalization * DISCOUNT_RATE
          investor_cash_currently_unconverted is:
          • PURCHASE_AMOUNT
          company_cash is:
          • company_cash_at_liquidity_event

        Go to Investor to Choose Method of Payment

    • Company action Initiate a Change-of-Control Liquidity Event

        Company must provide:
        • company_cash_at_liquidity_event, which is a USD amount
        • liquidity_capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • company_valuation, which is a positive USD amount
        • total_investments_of_cashout_investors, which is a positive USD amount
        • reduction_needed_to_qualify_as_usa_tax_free_reorg, which is a USD amount

        Define:
          conversion_price is:
          • the minimum of VALUATION_CAP / liquidity_capitalization and company_valuation / liquidity_capitalization * DISCOUNT_RATE
          investor_Common_Stock is:
          • ( investor_Common_Stock + ⎡ PURCHASE_AMOUNT / total_investments_of_cashout_investors * reduction_needed_to_qualify_as_usa_tax_free_reorg / conversion_price'⎤ )
          investor_cash_currently_unconverted is:
          • ( PURCHASE_AMOUNT - PURCHASE_AMOUNT / total_investments_of_cashout_investors * reduction_needed_to_qualify_as_usa_tax_free_reorg )
          company_cash is:
          • ( company_cash_at_liquidity_event - reduction_needed_to_qualify_as_usa_tax_free_reorg )

        Go to Investor to Choose Method of Payment

    • Company action Stock Payment Option Chosen by Default

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Common Stock
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Investor action Choose Payment in Common Stock

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Common Stock
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Transfer Common Stock

        Define:
          investor_Common_Stock is:
          • ( investor_Common_Stock + ⎡ ( investor_cash_currently_unconverted - liq_cashout ) / conversion_price⎤ )

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Execute Liquidity Event
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Investor action Choose Payment in Cash

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Cash (liquidity)
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Transfer Cash (liquidity)

        Company must provide:
        • total_investments_of_cashout_investors, which is a positive USD amount

        Define:
          liq_cashout is:
          • the minimum of investor_cash_currently_unconverted and company_cash * PURCHASE_AMOUNT / total_investments_of_cashout_investors
          investor_cash is:
          • ( investor_cash + liq_cashout' )

      • Scenario:

        • if liq_cashout is less than investor_cash_currently_unconverted
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Transfer Common Stock
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.
        • if liq_cashout = investor_cash_currently_unconverted
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Execute Liquidity Event
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Execute Liquidity Event

        Contract fulfilled.

  • Dissolution Events

    • Company action Initiate a Dissolution Event

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Cash (dissolution)
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Transfer Cash (dissolution)

        Company must provide:
        • company_cash_at_dissolution_event, which is a USD amount
        • investor_percent_of_dissolving_investor_investments, which is a fraction in the range (0,1]

        Define:
          dis_cashout is:
          • the minimum of PURCHASE_AMOUNT and company_cash_at_dissolution_event * investor_percent_of_dissolving_investor_investments
          investor_cash is:
          • dis_cashout'

      • Scenario:

        • if dis_cashout is less than PURCHASE_AMOUNT
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Transfer Assets
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.
        • if dis_cashout = PURCHASE_AMOUNT
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Execute Dissolution
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Transfer Assets

        Company must provide:
        • liquidity_capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • company_valuation, which is a positive USD amount

        Define:
          conversion_price is:
          • the minimum of VALUATION_CAP / liquidity_capitalization and company_valuation / liquidity_capitalization * DISCOUNT_RATE
          investor_Common_Stock is:
          • ( investor_Common_Stock + ⎡ ( PURCHASE_AMOUNT - dis_cashout ) / conversion_price'⎤ )

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Execute Dissolution
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action Execute Dissolution

        Contract fulfilled.
