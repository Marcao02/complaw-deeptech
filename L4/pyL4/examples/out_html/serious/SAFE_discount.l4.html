Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE)

The parameters to the contract are:
  • Purchase Amount, which is a positive USD.
  • Discount Rate, which is a fraction in the range (0,1].
  • Discount, which is a fraction in the range [0,1).
The contract tracks the following variables:
  • Change in Investor's Common Stock, which is a change in number of shares with initial value 0.
  • Change in Investor's SAFE Stock, which is a change in number of shares with initial value 0.
  • Change in Investor's Cash, which is a change in USD with initial value 0.
  • Unconverted Investment, which is a USD with initial value 0.
  • Conversion Price, which is a positive price in USD/share.
  • Change in Company's Cash, which is a USD with initial value 0.
  • company_cash_available_for_payout, which is a USD.
  • Liquidity Cashout, which is a USD with initial value 0.
  • Dissolution Cashout, which is a USD.

  1. Scenario: Investment Agreement Signed

    • Investor may Transfer Investment to Company

  2. Scenario: Investment Waiting to Convert

    • Company may Initiate an {Equity_Financing} Event
    • Company may Initiate an IPO Liquidity Event
    • if Total Investments of Cashout Investors is at least Purchase Amount
      • Company may Initiate a Change-of-Control Liquidity Event
    • Company may Initiate a Dissolution Event

  3. Investor action: Transfer Investment to Company

      Reduce Change in Investor's Cash by Purchase Amount
      Increase Change in Company's Cash by Purchase Amount
      Unconverted Investment is Purchase Amount

      Go to Investment Waiting to Convert

  4. Equity Events

    • Scenario: After PRA Signed

      • Within 1 day of the last event:
        • Company may Issue SAFE Preferred Stock
      • After 1 day since the last event:
        • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Initiate an {Equity_Financing} Event

      • Scenario:

        • Company may Deliver Transaction Docs With Pro Rata Rights Agreement (PRA)
        • Company may Deliver Transaction Docs (PRA to follow)
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Deliver Transaction Docs With Pro Rata Rights Agreement (PRA)

        Go to After PRA Signed

    • Company action: Deliver Transaction Docs (PRA to follow)

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Send PRA for Signing
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Send PRA for Signing

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Investor may Sign PRA
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Investor breaches the contract.

    • Investor action: Sign PRA

        Go to After PRA Signed

    • Company action: Issue SAFE Preferred Stock

        Company must provide:
        • Capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • Valuation, which is a positive USD

        Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price is Valuation / Capitalization
        Conversion Price is Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price * Discount Rate
        Increase Change in Investor's SAFE Stock by ⎡ Purchase Amount / conversion_price'⎤

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Execute {Equity_Financing}
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Execute {Equity_Financing}

        Contract fulfilled.

  5. Liquidity Events

    • Scenario: Investor to Choose Method of Payment

      • Before 1 week since the last event:
        • Investor may Choose Payment in Cash
      • Before 1 week since the last event:
        • Investor may Choose Payment in Common Stock
      • At 1 week since the last event:
        • Go to Stock Payment Option Chosen by Default

    • Company action: Initiate an IPO Liquidity Event

        Company must provide:
        • Capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • Valuation, which is a positive USD

        Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price is Valuation / Capitalization
        Conversion Price is Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price * Discount Rate

        Go to Investor to Choose Method of Payment

    • Company action: Initiate a Change-of-Control Liquidity Event

        Company must provide:
        • Capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • Valuation, which is a positive USD
        • Total Investments of Cashout Investors, which is a positive USD
        • Reduction Needed to Qualify as USA Tax-free Reorganization, which is a USD

        Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price is Valuation / Capitalization
        Conversion Price is Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price * Discount Rate
        Investor Cashout % is Purchase Amount / Total Investments of Cashout Investors
        Purchase Amount Reduction is Investor Cashout % * Reduction Needed to Qualify as USA Tax-free Reorganization
        Increase Change in Investor's Common Stock by ⎡ Purchase Amount Reduction / conversion_price'⎤
        Unconverted Investment is ( Unconverted Investment - Purchase Amount Reduction )
        Change in Company's Cash is ( Change in Company's Cash - Purchase Amount Reduction )

        Go to Investor to Choose Method of Payment

    • Company action: Stock Payment Option Chosen by Default

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Common Stock
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Investor action: Choose Payment in Common Stock

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Common Stock
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Transfer Common Stock

        Increase Change in Investor's Common Stock by ⎡ ( Unconverted Investment - Liquidity Cashout ) / Conversion Price⎤

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Execute Liquidity Event
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Investor action: Choose Payment in Cash

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Cash (liquidity)
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Transfer Cash (liquidity)

        Company must provide:
        • Total Cash Reserves, which is a USD
        • Total Investments of Cashout Investors, which is a positive USD

        Investor Cashout % is Purchase Amount / Total Investments of Cashout Investors
        Liquidity Cashout is the lesser of Unconverted Investment and Total Cash Reserves * Investor Cashout %
        Increase Change in Investor's Cash by liq_cashout'
        Unconverted Investment is ( Unconverted Investment - Liquidity Cashout )
        Change in Company's Cash is ( Change in Company's Cash - liq_cashout' )

      • Scenario:

        • if Liquidity Cashout is less than Unconverted Investment
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Transfer Common Stock
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.
        • if Liquidity Cashout = Unconverted Investment
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Execute Liquidity Event
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Execute Liquidity Event

        Contract fulfilled.

  6. Dissolution Events

    • Company action: Initiate a Dissolution Event

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Transfer Cash (dissolution)
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Transfer Cash (dissolution)

        Company must provide:
        • Total Cash Reserves, which is a USD
        • Investor Cashout %, which is a fraction in the range (0,1]

        Dissolution Cashout is the lesser of Purchase Amount and Total Cash Reserves * Investor Cashout %
        Change in Investor's Cash is dis_cashout'

      • Scenario:

        • if Dissolution Cashout is less than Purchase Amount
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Transfer Assets
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.
        • if Dissolution Cashout = Purchase Amount
          • Within 1 day of the last event:
            • Company may Execute Dissolution
          • After 1 day since the last event:
            • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Transfer Assets

        Company must provide:
        • Capitalization, which is a positive number of shares
        • Valuation, which is a positive USD

        Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price is Valuation / Capitalization
        Conversion Price is Undiscounted Preferred Stock Price * Discount Rate
        Increase Change in Investor's Common Stock by ⎡ ( Purchase Amount - Dissolution Cashout ) / conversion_price'⎤

      • Scenario:

        • Within 1 day of the last event:
          • Company may Execute Dissolution
        • After 1 day since the last event:
          • Company breaches the contract.

    • Company action: Execute Dissolution

        Contract fulfilled.
